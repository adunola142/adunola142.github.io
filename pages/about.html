Who I am

About Me

Mobile engineer by craft, problem-solver by nature — building apps that people rely on every day.

I'm Adeyemi Misturah, a Mobile App Engineer based in Lagos, Nigeria with 6+ years building products people actually use. I specialise in Flutter (Dart) and Native Android (Kotlin), with a strong track record in fintech, POS systems, and e-commerce.

My work spans the full spectrum — from architecting Bloc/Cubit state management in Flutter, to building the NestJS backends that power those apps, to shipping them to the Play Store and App Store. I care about reliability, clean architecture, and user experience.

I've contributed to apps used by 5,000+ active users, built secure POS hardware integrations with Mifare smart card tap-and-pay, and developed Notification-as-a-Service platforms delivering SMS, WhatsApp, and email at scale.

When I'm not building apps, I'm deepening my knowledge of cloud infrastructure and keeping up with the Flutter ecosystem.
